## Image Requirements

- Format: JPG, PNG, or GIF
- Size: Square format (e.g., 400x400px) for best display
- Quality: High quality for professional appearance
- Naming: Use descriptive names in English or Persian

## Upload Instructions

1. Upload your product images to this folder
2. Update the image paths in the database or admin panel
3. Ensure images are optimized for web (compressed but high quality)

The website will use placeholder images from Pexels if product images are not found.
